OVERTONE

One of my more ambitious projects has been writing a science fiction/fantasy novel that I have called "Overtone".  I started in the fall of '02 and have made a lot of progress.  I set the design process up very carefully so that I could pick it up and set it down whenever I am so inspired.  It may take years to actually complete, but it keeps my brain working, and relieves a lot of stress.

Synopsis:

A futuristic research experiment endows two men with the ability to see that the ãnaturalä world is more supernatural than had ever been imagined.  Follow them as they each take different paths through this world of living patterns, powers, and destinies, finding themselves on opposite sides of an unseen Universal struggle.
