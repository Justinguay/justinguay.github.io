About the Author

I am a servant of Jesus Christ, Husband of a lovely woman, and a Molecular Technician at a small college.

My first priority in Life is to serve the Lord. I was born in the state of Maine, and raised in the Catholic Church. When I was around the age of 8 I went to "Camp Fair Haven" and there for the first time I met my Lord and Savior. Later when I was about 12 my family grew dissatisfied with our current denomination, because of some teachings that seemed to conflict with the scriptures. As a family we decided to change denomination and from then on attended a non-denominational church, very similar to the Evangelical Free tradition. There we all grew in faith and followed the Lord together.

It was in that church that I met my sweetheart Nancy. We were both of the same age and both home schooled. After knowing each other for a little more than a year we decided to actively pursue a relationship with each other. Although now I laugh at some of the silly rules we made, and steps we took, I can really see God's leading in our relationship keeping us pure and building our friendship on a solid foundation. That was a long time ago; now we are married and making our lives together and looking ahead to growing old and gray side by side.

Home schooled Christians are not very common as scientists, but I strongly feel that the Lord has given me a talent to learn, and blessed me with the opportunity for this calling. My goal is continue to work hard wherever the Lord leads me, and hopefully earn advanced degree’s that will allow me to teach and to do my own research. I sense an eventual call to ministry of some sort, most likely later in life, but I'm leaving that up to Him, and taking things one-step at a time.

In the middle of all of this I have many hobbies that I pursue. These include fly-fishing, hunting, hiking, writing, bonsai, web design, reading, and more. Most of these can be learned about on this site.

If you have questions about faith, Grove City College (where I graduated), or molecular biology, drop me a note at losthawken50@netscape.net, and I would be glad to share with you anything about my life.

God Bless,

Justin G.
